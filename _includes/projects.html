ELI5 Software & Technology

Software and Technology is full of complicated concepts. ELI5 stands for 'Explain Like I'm 5', which is a technique to explain something complicated in a way that a 5 year old could understand. I wanted to create a simple interface that would present 'ELI5' variants of explanations for complex software and technology concepts.
